BIOGRAPHY

Deadly and precise, Xayah is a vastayan revolutionary waging a personal war to save her people.
She uses her speed, guile, and razor-sharp Clean Cuts feather blades to cut down anyone who stands in her way.
Xayah fights alongside her partner and lover, RakanSquare Rakan, to protect their dwindling tribe and restore their race to her vision of its former glory.
